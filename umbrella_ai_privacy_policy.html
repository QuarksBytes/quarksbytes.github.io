Introduction

Quarks Bytes ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Umbrella AI mobile application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, disclosed, and protected when you use our App.

By downloading, installing, or using Umbrella AI, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

About Umbrella AI

Umbrella AI is an offline artificial intelligence application that enables users to run AI models directly on their Android devices. AI processing is intended to occur locally on the user's device without requiring account registration or cloud-based AI processing by Quarks Bytes.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

Umbrella AI does not require users to create an account and does not intentionally collect personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Postal address
  • Date of birth

User Content

Prompts, conversations, files, and AI interactions are processed locally on the device. Quarks Bytes does not receive, store, or access such content through the App.

Device Information

The App itself does not intentionally collect device identifiers or device information for its own purposes. However, third-party advertising providers may collect certain information as described below.

Advertising

Umbrella AI displays advertisements through third-party advertising networks.

Advertising providers may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Advertising identifiers
  • Device information
  • IP address
  • Approximate location derived from IP address
  • Advertisement interaction information
  • Diagnostic information

This information is collected and processed by the advertising provider in accordance with its own privacy practices.

How We Use Information

Information collected by third-party advertising services may be used for:

Quarks Bytes does not use personal information for account creation, profiling, or direct marketing.

Local Processing

Umbrella AI is designed to perform AI inference locally on supported Android devices whenever possible.

User prompts and AI-generated responses are intended to remain on the user's device and are not transmitted to Quarks Bytes for processing.
