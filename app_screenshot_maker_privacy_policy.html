Introduction

Quarks Bytes ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Screenshot Maker mobile application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, disclosed, and protected when you use our App.

About Screenshot Maker

Screenshot Maker is an Android application that enables users to create and edit screenshots directly on their devices. Image creation and editing is performed locally on the user's device and does not require account registration or cloud-based processing by Quarks Bytes.

Screenshot Maker does not require users to create an account and does not intentionally collect personal information such as:
