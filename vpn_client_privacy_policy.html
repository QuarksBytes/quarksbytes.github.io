Privacy Policy

VPN & SSH/SFTP Client

Last updated: June 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the VPN & SSH/SFTP Client app ("the App", "we", "us") handles your information. We built this app with a simple principle: your connection credentials and server configurations belong to you, not us.

01

Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. Specifically, we never access or store:

🔑 SSH Credentials

Hostnames, IP addresses, usernames, passwords, and private keys you enter are stored exclusively on your device using local storage. They are never sent to us.

🛡️ VPN Configurations

WireGuard configs, V2Ray share links, and server profiles are saved locally on your device only.

📡 Network Traffic

We have no visibility into the content of your VPN tunnels or SSH sessions. Traffic flows directly between your device and the servers you configure.

📍 Location & Usage Data

We do not collect analytics, crash reports, location data, or behavioral data about how you use the App.

02

Third-Party Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalised or contextual ads, including:

Device identifiers (GAID) Approximate location IP address Ad interaction data Device model & OS version

Ads are never shown while a VPN tunnel is active. This is a deliberate design choice to avoid interfering with your connection and to limit ad network visibility into your tunnelled traffic.

For full details on how Google uses this data, see the Google Privacy Policy and AdMob data practices.

03

Camera & Photo Library

The App requests access to your camera and photo library solely to scan QR codes containing VPN or SSH profile configurations. Images are processed on-device only — no photos or scan results are uploaded anywhere.

04

SSH Key Generation

RSA and Ed25519 key pairs are generated entirely on your device using the PointyCastle and cryptography packages. The private key never leaves your device. We have no access to any key material you generate.

05

Data Storage

All App data (server profiles, SSH hosts, VPN configs, settings) is stored locally on your device via Android/iOS secure local storage. Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data.

We do not operate any cloud sync, backup, or account system. Your data never leaves your device through us.

06

Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has used this App in a way that raises privacy concerns, please contact us.

07

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
